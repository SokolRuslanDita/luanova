About Lua Nova

At Lua Nova Salon & Spa, we strive to help you look and feel your best. We are a full-service salon and spa offering a wide variety of professional hair and advanced skincare services, as well as nail care and bridal services. We are conveniently located off Highway 494 and Rockford Road in Plymouth, Minnesota, near Wayzata, Minnetonka, and Medina.

Hair Salon

Our team of Hairstylists stay current on trends so they can offer the best haircuts, dimensional color applications, beautiful balayage, vivid shades, and gray coverage to ensure our guests are wearing a look they love.

Our Advanced Practice Estheticians have the credentials and experience to perform microneedling, dermaplaning, microdermabrasion, various facials, and hair removal services that support a glowing complexion.

Our Nails Technicians are detailed and diligent about safety and sanitation. Their services include nail care for all ranging from basic pedicures which are popular amongst men, to beautiful gel polish manicures that last for weeks without chipping.

We have decades of experience with all hair and skin types and look forward to helping you on your journey to your best self. Consultations are always complimentary.
